I collate events that concern physics and the climate crisis into three calendars. The first is a sustainability within physics calendar. This aims to share events that discuss how we can make the field of physics more sustainable. The second is a physics and its contribution to sustainability calendar. This aims to share events that discuss how physics can help improve the sustainability of the world. Finally, the third calendar is simply a calendar of environmental 'holidays'. If you have or know of events that could be added, please do share them.
